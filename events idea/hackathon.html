Hackathon

What is a Hackathon?
A hackathon is an event where participants work intensively on programming and technology projects within a set timeframe to solve problems or create new products.

Why Should Students Participate?
Participating in a hackathon helps students:

  • Enhance their problem-solving skills.
  • Gain practical experience in coding and project development.
  • Collaborate with peers and industry professionals.
  • Showcase their skills to potential employers.

How to Register:

  • Visit the event's official website.
  • Sign up using the registration form.
  • Join as an individual or with a team.
  • Submit any required preliminary ideas or profiles.
