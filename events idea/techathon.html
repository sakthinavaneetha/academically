Techathon

What is a Techathon?
A Techathon is a competition where participants focus on technology-related projects, emphasizing innovation, technical skills, and problem-solving.

Why Should Students Participate?
Participation in a Techathon offers students:

  • Hands-on experience with emerging technologies.
  • Opportunity to work on real-world challenges.
  • Chance to network with industry leaders and potential employers.
  • Ability to develop and showcase their technical expertise.

How to Register:

  • Visit the event's official page.
  • Sign up as an individual or as a team.
  • Submit a brief description of your project or prototype.
  • Wait for confirmation and further details.
