Ideathon

What is an Ideathon?
An Ideathon is an event where participants brainstorm and pitch innovative ideas to address real-world problems, focusing on creativity and feasibility.

Why Should Students Participate?
Participating in an Ideathon provides students with:

  • Opportunity to develop and pitch innovative ideas.
  • Exposure to real-world problems and potential solutions.
  • Chance to collaborate with other creative thinkers.
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts and potential investors.

How to Register:

  • Go to the event's official website.
  • Register as an individual or with a team.
  • Select a problem statement or theme to work on.
  • Submit your idea or concept for review.
